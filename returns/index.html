Returns, Refunds & Satisfaction Policy

If you’re not fully satisfied with your order, we’re here to help.

Email us at alex@lumafood.com and please include your order number or full name.

Refunds & Replacements

We will issue a refund or replacement if the item you ordered from our website arrives damaged or the incorrect item was sent.

Satisfaction Policy

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on our full-price orders if:

  • It’s less than 30 days since your parcel was delivered
  • It’s the first time you have ordered this flavour
  • You have not returned more than two items before

If your item meets this criteria, please note the following:

  • Email alex@lumafood.com to organise a return
  • You cover the shipping cost of the return to us
  • We will process a refund once we receive your item
  • Original order shipping costs are not refundable
