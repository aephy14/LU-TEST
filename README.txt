LumaFood SEO Blog Pack

What this adds:
- Real crawlable blog URLs under /blog/ (best for SEO).
- robots.txt + sitemap.xml in the site root.

Where to paste:
1) Copy this whole folder into your repo root (same level as your index.html).
2) Paste your article content into:
   - blog/2025-10-24-the-health-benefits-of-matcha/index.html
   - blog/2025-09-16-matcha-soda-vs-coffee-nz/index.html

Then deploy (Cloudflare Pages will pick it up automatically).
